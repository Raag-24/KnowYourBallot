I. Before You Vote (Preparation)*

Voter Registration:

Eligibility: Clearly state the age requirement, residency requirements, and any other criteria for voter registration. Explain how to register (online, in person, by mail).

Registration Deadline: Prominently display the deadline for voter registration for upcoming elections.

Voter ID: Explain the required identification documents for registration and at the polling place. Include examples.

Checking Registration Status: Provide a link or instructions on how voters can verify their registration status and polling place.

Change of Address: Explain the process for updating voter registration information if someone has moved.
